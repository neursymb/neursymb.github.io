Welcome to NeurSymb Lab

We are a research laboratory dedicated to advancing the field of Neuro-Symbolic Artificial Intelligence. Our work bridges the gap between neural networks and symbolic reasoning, creating AI systems that are both powerful and interpretable.

Our Mission: Develop AI systems that can learn from data like neural networks, reason logically like symbolic systems, and explain their decisions to humans, achieving the trifecta of accuracy, reliability, and interpretability.
