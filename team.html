Team Members

Researchers working on neuro-symbolic AI and related areas.

Dr. Nguyen Ha Thanh

Lab Director

Dr. Nguyen Ha Thanh is an Assistant Professor of AI and Machine Learning at VinUniversity and a researcher at ROIS Tokyo. He received degrees in Information Technology and Law from Vietnam National University, and a Ph.D. in Information Science from the Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology. He has research experience at the National Institute of Informatics and ROIS-DS in Japan. His research focuses on neuro-symbolic AI for institutional decision systems, combining large language models with knowledge representation and reasoning.

Profile

Dr. Trinh Van Giang

Head of Professional Excellence

Dr. Trinh Van Giang is a lecturer and researcher at Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology. He was a postdoctoral researcher with the LIRICA team at LIS from April 2022 to September 2024, and with the LIFEWARE team at INRIA Saclay from October 2024 to August 2025. He earned his Ph.D. from the Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology in 2021. His research focuses on theoretical computer science, symbolic AI, computational systems biology, Boolean networks, Petri nets, answer set programming, and abstract argumentation.

Profile

Dr. Vuong Thi Hai Yen

Head of Learning & Development

Dr. Vuong Thi Hai Yen is a researcher at DS&KTLab, VNU University of Engineering and Technology. She graduated from the Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology. Her research focuses on legal natural language processing, information retrieval, argumentation mining, and knowledge representation. She has published work on data mining, comparative opinion mining, and knowledge graph construction for Vietnamese legal cases. Her work supports the development of neuro-symbolic methods for legal AI, legal reasoning, and structured knowledge extraction.

About Our Team

Our team consists of experienced researchers with diverse backgrounds in AI, machine learning, symbolic reasoning, and domain-specific applications. We collaborate across institutions and bring together expertise from multiple disciplines to tackle the most challenging problems in neuro-symbolic AI.

Each team member brings unique perspectives and skills, contributing to our collective mission of advancing AI systems that are both powerful and interpretable. We are committed to fostering an environment of collaborative research, continuous learning, and innovation.
